Wiz-A-Thon
Wiz-A-Thon is a stage for truely remarkable magical occurence. Every year the magic of code changes millions of lives, by one of the world's most mysterious species. To reach this utopian destination these unique mamals will institinly embark on a magical journey from all corners of the world. This is the wizardosarous, or more commonly known, the developer. Eleven and a half months of hibernation has taken its toll, and the sun is harsh as these nocturnal creatures must greet daylight. For those this momentous migration is thier first; while for the select few the pilgrimage is a time-honored tradition.

The only constraint is time.
